About Me

My name is Daniel, and I am a lifelong fan of video games new and old! My personal goal for building this website was to brush up on my web design skills, as well as my writing and critique abilities. Video games are a medium I have always been passionate about and find much interest in their discussion and analysis. They fuse elements of film and music and add an interactive component to it, which I feel is an interesting discussion topic and allows for a lot of depth. The main call to action is to introduce, recommend, or disregard various video games to the audience. I also wish to put a spotlight on some older or more obscure games the average viewer may have never heard of.

To hear about some games I love, check out the Best Of page!

To hear about some games I'm not huge on, check out the Worst Of page!
