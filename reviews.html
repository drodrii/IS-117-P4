Metroid Prime: Remastered

As a first time player of this critically acclaimed game I had a excellent time with it. This is visually one of the nicest looking games on the Switch and it perfectly adapts the 2D Metroid formula I love to 3D. A real highlight of this game for me were the boss battles, each of which required you to defeat them in different, creative ways.

Rating: 9/10
