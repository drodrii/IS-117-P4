Fallout 76 (PC, 2017)

An online adaption of the Fallout series is a very exciting idea on paper, but unforunately due to severe crunch on the developers, was an incredibly underwhelming product that felt unfinished on release. 76 was cluttered with bugs and paywalled features that kept it from being the experience many had hoped for.
